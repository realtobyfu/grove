## Description (<=4000)

Grove is a focused knowledge workspace for capturing ideas, organizing them into meaningful structures, and turning scattered notes into deeper understanding.

Capture quickly:
- Save notes in seconds
- Paste links and keep source context
- Use quick capture workflows to reduce friction

Organize naturally:
- Group related ideas into Boards
- Connect notes, concepts, and references
- Browse by tags and relationships

Think better:
- Explore your knowledge graph
- Revisit and refine earlier thoughts
- Use conversation-style reflection tools to clarify what you know

Stay in control:
- Local-first by default
- Optional cloud sync support when enabled
- Optional AI features you can configure

Grove is built for students, researchers, builders, and anyone who wants a calmer, more connected way to think and learn.

Grove Pro is an auto-renewable subscription available as a yearly plan. Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation of purchase. The subscription renews automatically unless it is canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings.
